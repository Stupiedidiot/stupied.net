# Still Messing around with this system blehhh

*Nothing is worth living for. Not this stupid class, its' faggot ass teacher, nor this joke of a country.* The poor ventilation was definitely not helping with Pepper's mood. She'd been stuck in the library since this morning, looking for the book her teacher requested a 1000 word essay on. While trying to trace back to why she even bothered to stay, an unfamiliar humming filled the silence. It was a bittersweet melody. One that was stumbling across the darkness, unsure if it will ever see a sliver of light. For a moment Pepper forgot about everything around her and commented "That's a lovely tune".

Pepper turns around to find the source of the tune skimming through the book on bottom shelf beside her. "Ah, it's nothing. Just something I've been working on". Mopsy was always working on something. Everyday she'd finish breakfast before anyone else and would spent the entire afternoon in the living room fiddling around the piano. She probably did it hoping that no one would ever bother her which ironically drew Pepper to the opposite. "For the concert, I presume?".

"Ah, no. It's something.. for myself" There was a lot of hesitation in last two words. Like she wasn't quite sure if she was permitted to say it. As silence settles back in, the Blonde plucks out another book open and traced her hand against the text on the wilting paper. It was a book Pepper was vaguely familiar with since it was the one they read back in Junior High. Most of what was in it was a blur for Pepper because of dull she found all of it. Mopsy looked at each word intensely, as if the contents in the book wasn't already the same thing

Like how it claimed *he* saved us from the Americans.

Sometimes she wonders whether Mopsy recognizes the ridiculousness of it too, or if she was to polite to think it out loud. Compared to those grandiose reports, two girls scavenging the dusty library is barely even statistic. Never to be recorded in any book nor to be ruminated the pens dictating the narrative. Destined to become obsolete. It's a shame really. For a tune that nothing, it was really something.

"We should probably get something to eat"

"So you'll finally stop pouting". There

*Mopsy notices things*, Pepper mentally reminded herself that. "Okkkkayy what ever you say, Blondie". As they made their way out the dusty, Pepper thinks to herself. *Maybe, **nothing** is worth living for.*

The more I look into older Duck media, the more I understand Don Rosa's dislike for the Ducktales series. Unfortunately, this was the catalyst for my obsession of these lil guys so my opinion will be incredibly biased.

Aside from the wet-dogification of Donald, I really enjoy the reimagined personalities 

I love how the triplets have their own distinct personalities outside of "more competent than Donald"

It strikes a wonderful balance between modernization and paying tribute its source. It set out to introduce these 80~ year old characters to a newer generation and, based on my zoomer ass gushing over it, I believe it has done its job well.

The episode that debuted Huey, Louie, and Dewey. It also mentions Della Duck in passing with the defunct name of "Dumbella". Despite being mostly incomprehensible, [Clarence Nash's](https://youtu.be/pf79aivmKIE) voice has always scratched a certain part of my brain. It's really cute how Donald makes an attempt to maintain his composer and be patient with the boys. Although I wished the nephews had a personality outside of terrorizing Donald, this short serves as a good introduction to their dynamic and kind of a justification for Donald's stern approach to the boys.

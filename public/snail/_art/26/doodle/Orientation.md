During a discussion about integrals, our professor demonstrated how to get the volume of simple shapes and he'd graph out the 3D shapes you could get rotating flat planes. For someone who I'd imagine only ever drew for his lectures, they were pretty good drawings. It made me realize art is just as much as a mental skill as it is a physical one. Which explains why there are some people who have been drawing all their lives yet have so little improvement to show for it. Felt a bit sad sketching because, deep down, I know I'm part of that latter group.

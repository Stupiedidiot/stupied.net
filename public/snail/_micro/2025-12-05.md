Suppose Pete has been Micky's #1 hater since day one but he works best when his pitted against Goofy.  They honestly have a really fun dynamic. Pete's strictness and pessimism against Goofy's laid back and unwaivering love for the world.

Goofy doesn't even fight back. Any rude remark and unnecessarily ruthless acts gets brushed off. The most you'll get is Goofy making small vague comments. It isn't because Goofy lack self-awareness; He is fully aware of his incompetence. He just values the joy of others above being perceived well.

![Goofy and Max side-by-side smiling at each other](img/25/12-05-Goofs.jpg)

This is most clearest when he's with his son Max, who is a lot more quick-witted and apprehensive than his father. Goofy tries his absolute darndest to maintain Max's wonder and happiness. It's also really cute how Max mirrors the stuff Goofy does to make him feel better. Just the fact he'd learned to adjust to Goof's goofs is really sweet. Goofy related media has no right to make me this emotional. What the actual fuck.

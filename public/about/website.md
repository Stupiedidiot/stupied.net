# {{ page.title }}
A culmination of everything in my being. There are certain pages titled as "Archive" but that word really applies to just about everything featured in this website. It is a digital record of my creations, fixations, and future. It is perpetually a work in progress with a hope that one day it will outlive me.

## Algorithms
> I don't like having my interests be dictated by algorithms. I want to be more deliberate about how I spend my time on the internet and what to leave behind. 

To elaborate further on my quote, I don't think algorithms are inherently bad. In fact, I spend quite some time studying them myself! Algorithms are just apathetic rulers that values numbers over social impact. It curates narrow views that too many people passively adopt regardless of it's accuracy. There are too many beautiful things in this world obfuscated by algorithms. Which is why this website exists! It is my way of actively seeking and promoting those beautiful things.

## Minimalism
It's incredibly difficult to decided what this website ought to be. As an archive, it should be reasonable to navigate and follow proper webdev conventions. As an artistic form of self-expression, it should be free to change on my whims and break a few boundaries. I've come to approach this website's design as a minimalist maximalist. Like, it's slightly above what you'd consider as minimalism but not enough to call maximalist. So really, average and that's okay. Generally, the indie web has a "fuck modern internet and their minialistic web design" mentality but I believe there is a good reason why those design philosophies prevailed. I don't mind if this website comes off as 'boring' compared to other websites. I just need it to, you know, work.

## Social Media
Just like literally every other person in the indieweb, I dislike social media and everything horrible that comes along with it. The need to adhere to fleeting trends, the blurred line of advertisements, and the constant stream of dubious information gives me a lot anxiety. I use a lot of methods to minimize it from my life. These include blocking sites through my [host file](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hosts_(file)), setting timers for certain apps, and using [Ublock](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/UBlock_Origin) to hide distracting UI elements. Needless to say, attempting to contact me through them will likely be futile. I am unfortunately a difficult person to get a hold of and it is by deliberate choice. Sorry...! :'D
